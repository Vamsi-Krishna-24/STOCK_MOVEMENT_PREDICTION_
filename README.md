<h2>🌳 Random Forest Magic: Decoding Stock Movements</h2>
<p>Enter the Random Forest Classifier, a team of stock analysts each giving their opinion. The final decision is like taking a vote - whatever most analysts agree on becomes the prediction. Introduced by Leo Breiman in 2001, this method mimics a panel of experts predicting market movements. 📊 (Source: Breiman, L. (2001). Random forests. Machine learning, 45(1), 5-32.)</p>

<p>Dockerizing is like packing a toolbox with everything needed for predicting stocks – codes, apps, and more – into a single, easy-to-move container.
